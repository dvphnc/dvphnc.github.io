Fundamentals

The tool that turns traffic into art

Every great pentester has one tool they can’t live without. For web application security, that tool is Burp Suite.

It’s not just a proxy. It’s not just a scanner. It’s the complete platform that lets you see, touch, and reshape every byte flowing between browser and server — like a producer sitting at the mixing desk of the internet.

“If you can see it, you can break it.
If you can break it, you can fix it.”

The Core Tools

Burp Suite is built around five legendary components:

  • Proxy — intercept everything
  • Repeater — tweak and resend forever
  • Intruder — automate the chaos
  • Scanner — find bugs while you sleep (Pro only)
  • Sequencer — test if randomness is real

Proxy: Your Invisible Middleman

Set your browser to 127.0.0.1:8080 and suddenly you’re in the room. Every request, every response, every cookie — you see it before it leaves your machine.

GET /admin HTTP/1.1
Host: vulnerable-lab.com
Cookie: session=eyJ1c2VyIjo...
User-Agent: Mozilla/5.0...

Repeater: The Loop Pedal

Found something interesting? Right-click → “Send to Repeater”. Now change one character and hit Go. Again. And again. Until the app finally tells you its secrets.

Intruder: When Manual Isn’t Enough

Need to test 10,000 passwords? Fuzz every parameter? Brute-force a hidden API endpoint? Intruder does it with four attack types: Sniper, Battering Ram, Pitchfork, and the infamous Cluster Bomb.

Sequencer: Entropy Never Lies

Capture 5,000 session tokens and watch Burp calculate the real randomness. Anything under 80 bits of entropy is basically a “please hack me” sign.
