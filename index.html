About

I'm Joana Daphne Sy, a 3rd Year Information Technology student who lives at the intersection of clean engineering and beautiful design. My goal is simple: build things that actually feel good to use.

On the development side, I architect full-stack Laravel applications and craft pixel-perfect frontend experiences. I'm the engineer who re-animates a button at 2AM because the easing curve was wrong, and I wouldn't have it any other way.

On the security side, I document exploits, run CTFs, and dig into how systems fail — because you can't protect what you don't understand. SyberVault is where that knowledge lives.

Currently working toward the ISC2 CC exam (June 2026) and deep in Figma auto-layout experiments on the side.
