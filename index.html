Hi — I'm Joana Daphne Sy, going by dvphnc online. I'm a 3rd-year IT student based in Quezon City who's genuinely obsessed with the overlap between building and breaking.

Half my time goes into full-stack web development — writing clean Laravel backends, crafting React interfaces that hold together under pressure, and caring way too much about whether a button hover feels right.

The other half goes into cybersecurity: running CTF challenges, documenting vulnerabilities, and working through the ISC2 CC curriculum the slow, methodical way. No AI-generated summaries. Just the source material, a terminal, and too much coffee.

I document everything in SyberVault — my public knowledge base for security concepts, exploit walkthroughs, and things I want to actually remember.
